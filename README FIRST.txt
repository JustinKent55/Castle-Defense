CASTLE DEFENSE


1.) Creeps will start by spawning at the left gate.
2.) Creeps will not spawn at the north gate until level 5.
3.) Creeps will not spawn at the south gate until level 6.
4.) Air creeps do not start spawning until level 8.


Notes: 
Right click to cancel placing a tower (Not currently shown by UI).
For this current version, the ground/air tower will not target or damage air creeps.
Future development needed to persist player's scores through the server.
